Welcome to Citumang

Your gateway to adventure in Pangandaran

Nature's Playground

Citumang is a breathtaking natural wonder located in the heart of Pangandaran, West Java. With its crystal-clear river waters, lush green surroundings, and thrilling adventure activities, it's the perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Whether you're looking for an adrenaline rush or a peaceful escape into nature, Citumang offers something for everyone. Our experienced guides ensure your safety while providing an unforgettable adventure experience.
